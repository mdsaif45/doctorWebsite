3. Dr. Fouad. M. Abbas, M.D.

Gynecologic Oncology

Dr. Abbas is a well-known Gynecologist/Oncologist. His specialization field is Oncologist of Obstetrician and Gynecology. He is also considered to be one of the best doctors of Oncology in the world. The term Oncology is the study of cancer. Dr. Abbas graduated from the University of Maryland School of Medicine in 1986. Currently, he is affiliated with Medstar Harbor Hospital and Sinai Hospital Of Baltimore. Dr. Abbas has received many awards including the CREOG National Faculty Award in resident education and was also awarded the Top Doctor by Baltimore Magazine.
